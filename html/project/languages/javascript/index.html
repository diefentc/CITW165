Food Per Day

Fetch by WebMD says that cats should eat between 24 to 35 calories a day, per pound

In order to find out how much your cat should be eating, we need to figure out their Resting and Maintenance Energy Requirements (RER and MER)

Maintenance Energy Requirements (MER)

Vets determine a cat's caloric needs in the following manner

Determine the Resting Energy Requirements

RER = 70(body weight in kg)0.75

Determine the MER

Determine this value by multiplying by an appropriate factor.
Typical Neutered pet: 1.2
Needs to lose weight: 0.8
The MER will be how many calories your cat will need for daily intake to either maintain or lose weight.

NOTE:For these calculations, I am assuming the pets are neutered and we are using 0.9 and 0.75 respectively
